Shakshouka

Description

This recipe will give you the perfect weeknight meal, full of protein with easy prep. Eggs cooked in a tangy and spicy tomato sauce feel eggs-cellent!

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 medium sized onion, diced
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 bell pepper, finely diced
  • 2 jalapenos, finely diced
  • salt to taste
  • black pepper to taste
  • Parsely to garnish
  • 2 slices of bread, toasted

Steps

  1. In a flat saucepan or skillet, add the oil and heat it up.
  2. Once the oil is heated, sautee the onions till translucent.
  3. Add the tomato paste and stir it in. Take care not to burn it.
  4. Add the jalapenos and bell pepper and sautee them well.
  5. Add the chopped tomatoes, and let them cook until mushy.
  6. Add water as required. We need to more a consistent tomato sauce to cook our eggs in.
  7. Once the sauce has cooked, spread it flat throughout the pan and make 4 gaps in the sauce. This is where we will place our eggs.
  8. Crack each egg and fill it in the gap. Once all eggs are placed, cover the saucepan and let it cook on low flow for a minute or so.
  9. Check on your eggs with a spoon, if the consistency of the egg is as per your taste, turn off the flame.
  10. Garnish with some parsely and serve along with toasted bread. Enjoy!
